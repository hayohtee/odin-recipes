Hot Dog


Description

A hot dog is a food consisting of a grilled or steamed sausage served in the slit of a partially sliced bun. The term hot dog can also refer to the sausage itself. The sausage used is a wiener or a frankfurter. The names of these sausages also commonly refer to their assembled dish.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Finely sliced onions
  • Dry cider
  • Brown sugar
  • Cider vinegar
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise
  • Sausages
  • Hot dog buns

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in a pan with the olive oil.
  2. Add the onions and a pinch of salt and fry for 25-30 mins or until caramelized.
  3. Add the cider and simmer until reduced. Stir in the sugar and vinegar.
  4. Mix the mustard with the mayonnaise.
  5. Cook the sausages following pack instructions.
  6. Split the hot dog buns and fill each with a sausage.
  7. Top with the onions and the mustard-mayo.
